# **App Name**: Khel Khoj

## Core Features:

- Video Recording: Allow athletes to record videos of their performance in the prescribed fitness assessment tests.
- AI-Powered Form Analysis: Analyze videos on-device to provide feedback on athlete form, such as jump height or sit-up posture, acting as a tool for proper assessment and anomaly detection. Output should focus only on analysis and relevant measurement, leaving yes/no fraud judgments to SAI.
- Automated Test Segmentation: Use AI to automatically detect and segment performance clips (e.g., counting reps in sit-ups or analyzing vertical jumps).
- Performance Benchmarking: Compare athlete performance against age/gender-based benchmarks and provide instant feedback.
- Offline Video Analysis: Perform preliminary performance analysis directly on the device without requiring continuous internet connectivity.
- Secure Data Submission: Securely submit verified data to SAI servers for further evaluation and athlete profiling.
- Gamified User Interface: Use progress badges, leaderboards, and interactive visuals to engage athletes and encourage participation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF7900) to evoke energy, passion, and enthusiasm, mirroring the spirit of sports.
- Background color: Light, desaturated orange (#FFEAD3) to provide a warm and inviting backdrop without distracting from the content.
- Accent color: Analogous yellow (#FFC300), brighter than the primary orange, to highlight key interactive elements and call to action.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, providing a precise and contemporary feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, balancing modernity with warmth for readability.
- Use clean, modern icons representing different sports and activities.
- Subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and enhance user engagement.
